Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum meets students’ needs.

At Washington University Nursery School, our educators are dedicated to monitoring and supporting each child's development through continuous observation and assessment. We understand that every student has unique learning needs, which is why our curriculum is dynamic and adaptable.

We specialize in tailoring our educational approaches to enhance individual learning experiences. This may involve:

  • Personalized Attention: Offering more one-on-one teacher-student time to understand the child’s thinking process, build trust, and increase student confidence.
  • Enhanced Exploration: Offering more opportunities for exploration.
  • Activity Adaptation: Adjusting activities by either simplifying or expanding them to better suit individual learning styles and needs.
  • Sensory Engagement: Incorporating diverse sensory experiences to stimulate different cognitive pathways and support holistic learning.
  • Focused Group Activities: Planning individual, small group, and/or large group activities around a particular concept, skill, or area of concern.
